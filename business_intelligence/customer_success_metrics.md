# Customer Success Metrics Knowledge Base

## Purpose

This knowledge base defines the Customer Success Impact Assessment framework used by HP Customer Success Teams, Product Teams, Leadership Teams, and Executive Decision Intelligence Agents.

The objective is to measure:

* How the requirement impacts customer satisfaction
* How it affects adoption and usage
* How it influences retention
* How it improves renewal probability
* How it creates expansion opportunities
* How it improves employee experience
* How it contributes to long-term customer success

This framework generates a Customer Success Score used in executive decision-making.

---

# Customer Success Scoring Framework

Customer Success Score is calculated using weighted factors.

Formula:

Customer Success Score =

Satisfaction Impact

*

Adoption Impact

*

Retention Impact

*

Renewal Impact

*

Expansion Opportunity Impact

*

Employee Experience Impact

---

# Scoring Scale

Each factor is scored from 0 to 100.

Maximum Score = 100

Minimum Score = 0
