# HP Opportunity Cost & Business Risk Framework

## Purpose

This framework helps evaluate:

1. The business impact of NOT fulfilling a requirement.
2. The opportunities HP may lose if the request is delayed or rejected.
3. The business risks associated with fulfillment.
4. The operational risks associated with non-fulfillment.
5. The overall business exposure.
6. Leadership decision urgency.

The objective is to answer:

"What happens if HP does not fulfill this requirement?"

and

"What risks exist if HP fulfills or delays the request?"

---

# SECTION 1: OPPORTUNITY COST ASSESSMENT

## Definition

Opportunity Cost is the value of benefits, productivity, innovation, or business outcomes that HP loses when a requirement is delayed, rejected, or not fulfilled.

The Business Intelligence Agent must calculate:

- Cost of delay
- Cost of rejection
- Cost of inaction
- Lost productivity
- Lost innovation
- Lost business value

---

# SECTION 2: OPPORTUNITY COST CATEGORIES

## Productivity Loss

Definition:

Loss of employee productivity due to inadequate tools, systems, infrastructure, or resources.

Examples:

- AI engineer waiting for suitable hardware
- Slow development environment
- Delayed testing activities
- Manual workaround processes

Business Impact:

- Slower delivery
- Reduced output
- Missed deadlines

Scoring:

| Impact | Score |
|----------|-------|

| Medium | 10 |
| Low | 5 |

# SECTION 4: BUSINESS RISK ASSESSMENT

## Definition

Business Risk measures potential negative outcomes associated with fulfilling, delaying, or rejecting the requirement.

The Business Intelligence Agent must identify and evaluate risks.

---

# SECTION 5: BUSINESS RISK CATEGORIES

## Inventory Risk

Definition:

Risk caused by unavailable inventory.

Examples:

- Device shortage
- Spare part shortage
- Hardware allocation conflict

Impact:

- Fulfillment delays
- Escalations
- Resource constraints

Severity:

Low / Medium / High / Critical

---

## Procurement Risk

Definition:

Risk associated with acquiring required components.

Examples:

- Supplier delays
- Extended lead times
- Component shortages

Impact:

- Project delays
- Increased costs

Severity:

Low / Medium / High / Critical

---

## Vendor Dependency Risk

Definition:

Dependency on a limited number of suppliers.

Examples:

- Single GPU supplier
- Single hardware vendor
- Specialized component sourcing

Impact:

- Supply chain disruption
- Procurement bottlenecks

Severity:

Low / Medium / High / Critical

---

## Budget Risk

Definition:

Risk associated with exceeding budget limits.

Examples:

- High procurement cost
- Unplanned hardware upgrades
- Cost escalation

Impact:

- Budget overruns
- Approval delays

Severity:

Low / Medium / High / Critical

---

## Adoption Risk

Definition:

Risk that users may not fully utilize the investment.

Examples:

- Underutilized hardware
- Unused features
- Poor adoption

Impact:

- Reduced ROI
- Reduced value realization

Severity:

Low / Medium / High / Critical

---

## Supportability Risk

Definition:

Risk associated with maintaining and supporting the solution.

Examples:

- Specialized hardware support
- Complex troubleshooting
- Resource dependency

Impact:

- Increased support costs
- Longer resolution times

Severity:

Low / Medium / High / Critical

---

## Technology Obsolescence Risk

Definition:

Risk that technology may become outdated quickly.

Examples:

- Rapid AI hardware evolution
- Emerging technology shifts

Impact:

- Reduced lifespan
- Additional future investment

Severity:

Low / Medium / High / Critical

---

# SECTION 6: RISK SEVERITY MATRIX

| Severity | Description |
|-----------|------------|
| Critical | Immediate business impact, executive attention required |
| High | Significant business impact, mitigation required |
| Medium | Manageable impact, monitoring required |
| Low | Minimal impact, acceptable risk |

# SECTION 7: LEADERSHIP DECISION GUIDELINES

If:

- Opportunity Cost is High
- Business Value is High
- Strategic Alignment is Strong

Recommendation:

APPROVE

---

If:

- Opportunity Cost is Moderate
- Risks are Medium

Recommendation:

APPROVE WITH REVIEW

---

If:

- Opportunity Cost is Low
- Benefits are unclear

Recommendation:

HOLD

---

If:

- Opportunity Cost is Minimal
- Risks exceed benefits

Recommendation:

REJECT
