# Business Value Framework

## Purpose

This framework helps evaluate the overall business value of fulfilling a customer, employee, product, or operational requirement.

The framework is used to determine:

* Why the requirement matters to the business
* What benefits will be achieved
* Which teams will benefit
* Whether the investment is justified
* How much business impact the requirement creates

---

# Business Value Categories

## 1. Productivity Improvement

### Definition

Measures how much employee productivity increases after fulfilling the requirement.

### Indicators

* Reduced manual effort
* Faster task completion
* Faster decision making
* Improved employee output
* Reduced turnaround time

### Examples

* AI-ready laptop reduces model training setup time
* Better hardware reduces system waiting time
* Automation removes repetitive work

### Business Impact

High productivity improvement leads to:

* Faster project completion
* Better resource utilization
* Increased employee efficiency

### Scoring

| Impact Level | Score |
| ------------ | ----- |
| Very High    | 20    |
| High         | 15    |
| Medium       | 10    |
| Low          | 5     |

# Business Value Interpretation

## Exceptional Business Value (90-100)

Characteristics:

* Strong strategic importance
* Significant productivity gains
* Strong innovation impact
* Major business benefits

Leadership Recommendation:

APPROVE

---

## High Business Value (75-89)

Characteristics:

* Clear business benefits
* Positive operational impact
* Supports strategic objectives

Leadership Recommendation:

APPROVE

---

## Moderate Business Value (50-74)

Characteristics:

* Business value exists
* Benefits are measurable
* Additional validation may be required

Leadership Recommendation:

APPROVE WITH REVIEW

---

## Low Business Value (30-49)

Characteristics:

* Limited measurable benefit
* Weak strategic alignment
* Benefits uncertain

Leadership Recommendation:

HOLD FOR REVIEW

---

## Minimal Business Value (<30)

Characteristics:

* Poor justification
* Limited impact
* Weak alignment with objectives

Leadership Recommendation:

REJECT

---

# Executive Assessment Questions

The Business Intelligence Agent must answer:

1. What business problem is being solved?
2. Which teams benefit from this requirement?
3. How much productivity improvement is expected?
4. How much operational efficiency improvement is expected?
5. Does this support innovation or AI initiatives?
6. Does this improve employee experience?
7. Does this reduce costs?
8. What is the overall business value score?
9. What is the business value classification?
10. Should leadership approve the investment?
