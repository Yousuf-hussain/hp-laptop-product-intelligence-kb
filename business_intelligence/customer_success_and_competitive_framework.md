# HP Customer Success & Competitive Position Framework

## Purpose

This framework helps evaluate:

1. Customer Success Impact
2. Employee Success Impact
3. Adoption Potential
4. Productivity Improvement
5. User Experience Improvement
6. Competitive Positioning
7. Competitive Risks
8. Market Readiness

The objective is to determine:

- Will the requirement improve user success?
- Will the requirement improve productivity?
- Will the requirement improve satisfaction?
- Does HP remain competitive?
- What risks exist from competitors?

---

# SECTION 1: CUSTOMER SUCCESS ASSESSMENT

## Definition

Customer Success measures whether the fulfilled requirement creates measurable value for the end user.

For HP this may include:

- Internal Employees
- Engineers
- Developers
- AI Teams
- Analysts
- Product Teams
- Enterprise Customers

Customer Success is achieved when users:

- Accomplish objectives faster
- Experience fewer obstacles
- Improve productivity
- Increase adoption
- Achieve business outcomes

---

---

- Customer Success Score 50-74
- Competitive Position Score 50-74

Recommendation:

APPROVE WITH REVIEW

---

- Customer Success Score below 50
- Competitive Position Score below 50

HOLD

REJECT
