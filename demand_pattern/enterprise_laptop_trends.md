# Enterprise Laptop Demand Trends

## Purpose

This document captures enterprise laptop demand patterns, historical requirement trends, feature popularity, department-specific preferences, and emerging technology requirements.

The objective is to help AI agents identify recurring customer demand, prioritize requirements, support product recommendations, and assist business decision-making.

---

# Trend Classification Framework

## High Demand

Definition:

Requirements frequently requested across multiple departments and business units.

Criteria:

* Requested in 5 or more Jira tickets
* Multiple department adoption
* Strategic business relevance

Priority:

High

---

## Medium Demand

Definition:

Requirements requested periodically by specific departments or teams.

Criteria:

* Requested in 2–4 Jira tickets
* Department-specific usage

Priority:

Medium

---

## Low Demand

Definition:

Specialized or niche requirements.

Criteria:

* Requested in fewer than 2 Jira tickets
* Limited user base

Priority:

Low

---

Classification:

High Business Demand

Proceed with Feasibility Assessment

Moderate Business Demand

# Business Recommendation

Requirements aligned with high-demand trends should receive higher priority, faster feasibility assessment, and accelerated review processes.

Requirements aligned with emerging trends such as AI, machine learning, and enterprise security should be considered strategic opportunities and evaluated for future product roadmap inclusion.
