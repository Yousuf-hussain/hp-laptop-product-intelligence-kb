# Approval Matrix & Governance Framework

## Purpose

This document provides approval thresholds, governance rules, financial approval requirements, risk-based approvals, ROI-based approvals, procurement approvals, and escalation guidelines for HP laptop requirement fulfillment projects.

It is used by the Cost, Business Value, and ROI Analysis Agent to determine:

* Who should approve the request
* Whether approval is required
* Escalation level
* Financial approval requirements
* Risk approval requirements
* Business approval requirements
* Executive review requirements

The objective is to ensure that every laptop request is approved by the appropriate authority based on:

* Cost
* Risk
* Complexity
* ROI
* Strategic impact
* Procurement impact

---

# Approval Hierarchy

## Level 1

### Manager

Responsible For:

* Standard laptop requests
* Low-risk requests
* Existing inventory allocation
* Standard upgrades

Authority:

* Operational approval
* Budget approval within limits

---

## Level 2

### Senior Manager

Responsible For:

* Medium-cost requests
* Moderate complexity requests
* Procurement validation
* Resource allocation approval

Authority:

* Department-level approval

---

## Level 3

### Director

Responsible For:

* High-value requests
* Strategic requests
* AI workstation requests
* Cross-functional approvals

Authority:

* Business unit approval

---

## Level 4

### Vice President (VP)

Responsible For:

* Large investments
* Enterprise-wide initiatives
* High-risk strategic investments

Authority:

* Executive approval

---

## Level 5

### Executive Leadership

Responsible For:

* Major strategic investments
* Product portfolio decisions
* Significant budget allocations

Authority:

* Final executive decision

# Final Approval Decision Rules

## Auto Approve

Conditions:

* ROI > 100%
* Risk Low or Medium
* Cost < $5,000
* Inventory Available

Decision:

APPROVE

---

## Approve With Review

Conditions:

* ROI > 50%
* Medium Risk
* Procurement Required

Decision:

APPROVE WITH REVIEW

---

## Escalate To Director

Conditions:

* Cost > $5,000
* High Risk
* Strategic Requirement

Decision:

DIRECTOR REVIEW REQUIRED

---

## Escalate To VP

Conditions:

* Cost > $25,000
* Critical Risk
* Enterprise Impact

Decision:

VP APPROVAL REQUIRED

---

## Reject

Conditions:

* Negative ROI
* High Cost
* Low Business Value

Decision:

NOT RECOMMENDED

# Agent Usage Rules

The Cost & ROI Analysis Agent must:

1. Evaluate cost thresholds.
2. Evaluate ROI thresholds.
3. Evaluate risk levels.
4. Evaluate procurement impact.
5. Evaluate complexity.
6. Determine approval authority.
7. Generate approval recommendation.
8. Explain why approval is required.
9. Include approval outcome in Executive Summary.
