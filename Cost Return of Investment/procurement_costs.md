# Procurement Cost & Supplier Intelligence Catalog

## Purpose

This document provides procurement cost assumptions, supplier intelligence, lead-time estimates, shipping costs, sourcing risks, approval requirements, and procurement planning rules for HP laptop requirement fulfillment.

It is used by the Cost, Business Value, and ROI Analysis Agent to estimate:

* Procurement cost
* Supplier dependency risk
* Lead time impact
* Shipping cost
* Sourcing complexity
* Procurement approval level
* Supply chain risk
* Delivery timeline
* Budget impact

The objective is to help Procurement Teams, Customer Success Teams, Product Managers, and Management understand:

* What needs to be procured
* How much procurement will cost
* How long procurement will take
* What risks exist
* Whether inventory or procurement is the preferred option

---

# Procurement Cost Components

## Procurement Cost Formula

Total Procurement Cost =

Vendor Cost
+
Shipping Cost
+
Import / Logistics Cost
+
Vendor Management Cost
+
Contingency Cost

# Supplier Categories

## Tier 1 Suppliers

Definition:

Strategic HP-approved suppliers.

Characteristics:

* Long-term contracts
* Preferred vendors
* High reliability
* Predictable delivery
* Low supply risk

Examples:

* HP Enterprise Suppliers
* Authorized OEM Vendors

Risk Level:

Low

Approval Required:

Manager

---

## Tier 2 Suppliers

Definition:

Approved secondary suppliers.

Characteristics:

* Competitive pricing
* Moderate reliability
* Limited inventory guarantees

Risk Level:

Medium

Approval Required:

Senior Manager

---

## Tier 3 Suppliers

Definition:

Emergency or alternative suppliers.

Characteristics:

* High cost
* Variable quality
* Unpredictable availability

Risk Level:

High

Approval Required:

Director

# Procurement Complexity Classification

## Low Complexity

Conditions:

* Standard inventory component
* Single supplier
* Lead time < 7 days

Examples:

* RAM
* SSD

---

## Medium Complexity

Conditions:

* Multiple approvals required
* Lead time 7–14 days

Examples:

* Battery
* Fingerprint Hardware
* EliteBook

---

## High Complexity

Conditions:

* Limited suppliers
* Long lead time
* High budget

Examples:

* AI GPU
* ZBook AI Configuration
