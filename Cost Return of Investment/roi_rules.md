# ROI Rules & Business Value Calculation Framework

## Purpose

This document provides standard Return on Investment (ROI), Payback Period, Business Value, Productivity Gain, Opportunity Cost, Customer Success Impact, and Investment Decision rules for HP laptop requirement fulfillment projects.

It is used by the Cost, Business Value, and ROI Analysis Agent to answer:

* Is this requirement financially worth fulfilling?
* What business value will it generate?
* What is the ROI?
* What is the payback period?
* What happens if HP does not fulfill the requirement?
* What is the expected productivity gain?
* What is the Customer Success impact?
* Should management approve the investment?

---

# Core Business Principle

Investment should be approved only if:

Business Benefit > Total Cost

and

ROI is positive

and

Strategic Value is acceptable

and

Customer Success impact is positive.

---

# ROI Formula

## Standard ROI Formula

ROI (%) =

((Total Benefit - Total Cost) / Total Cost) × 100

---

## Example

Total Cost:

$3,000

Total Benefit:

$9,000

ROI =

((9000 - 3000) / 3000) × 100

ROI = 200%

---

# Net Benefit Formula

Net Benefit =

Total Benefit - Total Cost

---

## Example

Total Benefit:

$10,000

Total Cost:

$4,000

Net Benefit:

$6,000

---

# Payback Period Formula

Payback Period (Months) =

Total Cost / Monthly Benefit

---

## Example

Total Cost:

$3,000

Monthly Benefit:

$500

Payback Period:

6 Months

---

# Business Priority Score Rules

## Formula

Business Priority Score =

Customer Impact (25)
+
Strategic Value (25)
+
Demand Trend (20)
+
ROI (15)
+
Customer Success Impact (15)

Maximum Score = 100

---

| Score    | Priority |
| -------- | -------- |
| 90 - 100 | Critical |
| 75 - 89  | High     |
| 50 - 74  | Medium   |
| 25 - 49  | Low      |
| Below 25 | Very Low |

# Investment Decision Rules

## Automatically Recommend Approval

Conditions:

* ROI > 100%
* Payback < 12 Months
* Priority Score > 75
* Customer Success Impact High

Decision:

Approve

---

## Recommend Approval With Review

Conditions:

* ROI 50–100%
* Payback < 24 Months

Decision:

Approve With Review

---

## Recommend Hold

Conditions:

* ROI 0–50%
* Priority Medium

Decision:

Hold for Business Justification

---

## Recommend Reject

Conditions:

* Negative ROI
* Low Business Value
* Low Customer Impact

Decision:

Not Recommended

# Important Disclaimer

All ROI calculations are estimated planning models.

Actual business value may vary depending on:

* User adoption
* Workload intensity
* Technology utilization
* Market conditions
* Customer behavior

Final investment decisions should be validated by Finance, Product Management, Procurement, and Business Leadership teams.
