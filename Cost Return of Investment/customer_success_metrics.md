# Customer Success Impact Assessment Framework

## Purpose

This document provides a standardized framework for measuring Customer Success impact associated with fulfilling or rejecting a customer requirement.

The framework helps Customer Success Managers, Product Teams, Leadership Teams, and Management answer:

* How does this requirement affect customer satisfaction?
* Will this improve customer retention?
* Will this increase adoption?
* Will this create expansion opportunities?
* Will this improve future renewals?
* What is the overall Customer Success value?

The objective is to quantify Customer Success impact and include it in business and investment decisions.

---

# Customer Success Impact Formula

Customer Success Score =

Customer Satisfaction Impact
+
Retention Impact
+
Adoption Impact
+
Renewal Impact
+
Expansion Opportunity Impact

Maximum Score = 100

# Agent Usage Rules

The Cost & ROI Analysis Agent must:

1. Calculate Customer Success Score.
2. Assess satisfaction impact.
3. Assess retention impact.
4. Assess adoption impact.
5. Assess renewal impact.
6. Assess expansion opportunity impact.
7. Include Customer Success Score in Executive Dashboard.
8. Include Customer Success impact in final recommendation.
9. Use Customer Success Score as part of approval decisions.

---

# Important Disclaimer

Customer Success scores are strategic planning estimates intended to support decision-making.

Actual customer satisfaction, retention, adoption, renewal, and expansion outcomes may vary based on customer behavior, business priorities, technology adoption, and market conditions.
