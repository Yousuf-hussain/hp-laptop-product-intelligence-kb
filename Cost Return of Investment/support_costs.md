# Support Cost & Total Cost of Ownership Catalog

## Purpose

This document provides standard support, maintenance, warranty, replacement, monitoring, and lifecycle support cost assumptions for HP laptop deployments.

It is used by the Cost, Business Value, and ROI Analysis Agent to estimate:

* Support costs
* Maintenance costs
* Warranty costs
* Replacement costs
* Incident management costs
* Annual ownership costs
* Multi-year ownership costs
* Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)

The objective is to help Management, Customer Success Teams, Support Teams, Procurement Teams, and Product Teams understand the long-term operational cost of fulfilling a laptop requirement.

---

# Support Cost Categories

## Support Cost Formula

Annual Support Cost =

Incident Support Cost
+
Preventive Maintenance Cost
+
Monitoring Cost
+
Warranty Support Cost
+
Replacement Reserve Cost

# Customer Success Impact Rules

## Low Support Environment

Characteristics:

* Few incidents
* Stable configuration
* Low maintenance

Customer Success Impact:

High Satisfaction

---

## High Support Environment

Characteristics:

* Frequent incidents
* Complex AI workloads
* Multiple escalations

Customer Success Impact:

Medium Satisfaction

Recommendation:

Additional monitoring and proactive maintenance

# Agent Usage Rules

The Cost & ROI Analysis Agent must:

1. Calculate annual support cost.
2. Calculate maintenance cost.
3. Calculate warranty cost.
4. Calculate replacement reserve cost.
5. Calculate 3-year ownership cost.
6. Estimate long-term support burden.
7. Include support costs in ROI calculations.
8. Identify support risk classification.
9. Generate ownership cost analysis for management.

---

# Important Disclaimer

All support costs, maintenance costs, warranty costs, and replacement costs are planning estimates only.

Actual support costs may vary based on:

* User behavior
* Device usage
* AI workload intensity
* Hardware failures
* Warranty coverage
* Support agreements

Final support cost validation should be performed by Support Operations, Customer Success, and Finance Teams.
