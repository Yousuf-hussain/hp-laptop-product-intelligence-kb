# HP EliteBook Product Intelligence

## Product Family Overview

HP EliteBook is HP's premium enterprise business laptop family designed for executives, managers, consultants, business analysts, finance teams, customer success teams, and enterprise professionals who require security, reliability, mobility, and productivity.

The EliteBook series focuses on enterprise-grade security, lightweight design, long battery life, remote workforce enablement, and business productivity rather than workstation-class computing.
