# HP ProBook Product Intelligence

## Product Family Overview

HP ProBook is HP's business productivity laptop family designed for small businesses, enterprise operations teams, customer support teams, sales teams, and general business users who require reliability, security, affordability, and day-to-day productivity.

The ProBook series provides a balance between performance, cost, security, and manageability, making it suitable for large-scale enterprise deployments where high-end workstation capabilities are not required.
