# Dell Precision Competitor Intelligence

## Product Family Overview

Dell Precision is Dell's flagship mobile workstation product family designed for engineering, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, software development, simulation, rendering, CAD design, and other professional workloads requiring workstation-class performance.

Dell Precision is one of HP ZBook's primary competitors and is widely used by enterprises, engineering firms, research organizations, and technology companies.
