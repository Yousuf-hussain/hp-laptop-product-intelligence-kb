# Lenovo ThinkPad Competitor Intelligence

## Product Family Overview

Lenovo ThinkPad is Lenovo's flagship enterprise laptop family and one of the most recognized business laptop brands globally. ThinkPad devices are widely used by large enterprises, government organizations, financial institutions, consulting firms, and technology companies.

The ThinkPad family is known for reliability, durability, enterprise security, keyboard quality, and business productivity.
