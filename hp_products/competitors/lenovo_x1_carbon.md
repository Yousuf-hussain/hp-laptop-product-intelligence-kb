# Lenovo X1 Carbon Competitor Intelligence

## Product Family Overview

Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon is Lenovo's flagship premium enterprise ultrabook designed for executives, business leaders, consultants, sales professionals, and enterprise users who require exceptional mobility, long battery life, premium build quality, enterprise security, and productivity.

The X1 Carbon combines the reliability of the ThinkPad family with a lightweight premium design, making it one of the strongest competitors to HP Spectre and premium HP EliteBook models.
