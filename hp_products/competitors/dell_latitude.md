# Dell Latitude Competitor Intelligence

## Product Family Overview

Dell Latitude is Dell's flagship enterprise business laptop family designed for executives, managers, business professionals, consultants, sales teams, customer success teams, and enterprise users.

The Latitude series is one of HP EliteBook's primary competitors and is widely adopted by global enterprises due to its security, reliability, manageability, and enterprise support ecosystem.
