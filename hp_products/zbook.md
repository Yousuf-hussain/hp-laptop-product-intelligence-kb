# HP ZBook Product Intelligence

## Product Overview

HP ZBook is HP's flagship mobile workstation series designed for high-performance computing, artificial intelligence, machine learning, engineering, software development, data science, simulation, rendering, and professional creative workloads.

The ZBook family combines workstation-class processing power with enterprise security and mobility, making it the preferred choice for technical professionals requiring advanced CPU, GPU, memory, and storage capabilities.
